Weight Management and Healthy Living

Understanding Your BMI

Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is an indicator of body fat based on height and weight. Knowing your BMI can help you understand where you stand and how you can take steps toward a healthier lifestyle.

Tips to Maintain a Healthy Weight

  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Focus on including a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your meals.
  • Stay Active: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week, like brisk walking, cycling, or swimming.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking enough water is essential for overall health and can help manage appetite.
  • Get Enough Sleep: Poor sleep can disrupt metabolism and increase hunger. Aim for 7-9 hours each night.
  • Limit Processed Foods: Foods high in sugars and unhealthy fats can contribute to weight gain.
