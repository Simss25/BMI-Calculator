Weight Management Information

Weight Categories

Based on BMI, weight categories are classified as follows:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI of 30 or higher

Health Issues Related to Weight

Weight outside the normal range can increase risks for various health conditions:

  • Underweight: Risk of malnutrition, osteoporosis, anemia, and weakened immune function.
  • Overweight/Obesity: Increased risk of heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and joint issues.

Weight Management Tips

Here are some tips to manage and maintain a healthy weight:

  • Balanced Diet: Eat a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid excessive sugars and processed foods.
  • Regular Exercise: Engage in physical activity, like walking, jogging, or strength training, for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Adequate Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night to support overall health.
  • Stress Management: Practice mindfulness, meditation, or hobbies to reduce stress, which can impact weight.
